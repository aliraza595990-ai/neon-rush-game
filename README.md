## 🌟 Features
- 🚗 **10 Cars** — Sedan, Sports GT, Muscle Car, Police Cruiser, Ghost Racer & more
- 🏙️ **Open World** — Neo City with 5 Districts  
- 📋 **10 Missions** — Delivery, Racing, Chase, Heist, Boss Fight
- 🚔 **Police System** — 5 Wanted Levels + Helicopter
- ⚡ **Nitro System** — Speed Boost with Visual Effects
- 🛒 **Upgrade Shop** — Engine, Armor, Tires, Neon & more
- 💰 **Economy** — Earn Cash, Buy Cars, Upgrade
- 🗺️ **Minimap** — Real-time City Navigation
- 🌧️ **Rain Effects** — Beautiful Neon Rain Atmosphere
- 💾 **Auto Save** — Progress Saves Automatically

## 🕹️ Controls
| Mobile | PC |
|--------|-----|
| Left/Right Arrows | A/D or arrow keys |
| Gas Button ▲ | W or ↑ |
| Brake Button ▼ | S or ↓ |
| Nitro ⚡ | N key |
| Handbrake 🔒 | Space |

## 📖 Story
You are **Zain** — a Street Racer in Neo City. Corrupt Police Chief Rex Donovan impounded your car. Fight back, earn money, buy the best cars, and take down Rex!
